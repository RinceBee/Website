About me ISFJ

Hi, I am Kumar Baral. I am from Ilam. My fascination with engineeering is not an old one. I can still vividly recall the school day when I saw engineer wearing a white cap in building construction works near my home town. The engineer, supervising the workers and taking the responsibility of the construction works, was almost charismatic and glamorous in a way. The fact that engineer is the one with the most contribution to the society and the respect he gets from the people around him triggered my interest in the subject. I Joined undergraduate studies in Pulchowk Campus with scholarship in 2008.

After the completion of my undergraduate studies where I studied core engineering subjects. However, I was more gravitated toward Water Science and Engineering. So that, I joined a firm Hydro-consult Engineering Limited, which basically works on the field of water; water management, policies and majorly in hydropower development.

After working there for few years, my curiosity increased more and I joined IHE Delft Institute of Water Education to acquire in-depth knowledge in Water Resource Engineering in 2018 in the Netherlands. There, I studied Hydrology, Hydraulics, Dam and Hydropower and Resource Management too.

After being graduate of water resource engineering, I returned in Nepal an joined Hydro Plan Engineering Pvt. Ltd. After working there for 2 years, I again joined Hydro-Consult Engineering Limited.
