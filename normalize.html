Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Founded in 1987 by the Hernandez brothers, our establishment has been serving up rich coffee sourced from artisanal roasters, baked goods, and lunch offerings for over 30 years. With humble beginnings in a small town, our cafe has grown to be a staple of the community, known for our warm atmosphere and delicious fare.

We believe that serving up a quality cup of coffee shouldn't come at the cost of the environment. That's why we source our beans from sustainable, small-scale farmers and use eco-friendly packaging. Come visit us for a cup of coffee, a bite to eat, and to support a business dedicated to making a difference in the world.
